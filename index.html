Who we are.

Welcome to G-Tree Cafe. With quick, friendly and attentive service plus a delicious menu, it's the perfect spot for a coffee (locally roasted) or bite to eat. Whether you’re holding a business meeting or catching up with friends, the café’s spacious interior is ideal for large groups, with many tables available. The café offers outdoor and indoor seating, so you can make the most of good weather, or huddle inside if it’s cold. The mouth-watering menu offers generous portions and a wide variety of dishes. But what really sets G-Tree apart is the warm and friendly service you’ll receive. You can expect fast and attentive service with a smile.

Organic Coffee

Check out the huge breakfast menu which includes a Stockman's Style Breaky, Buttermilk Pancakes, or Portobello with eggs. For those with a sweet tooth, try the Morning Apple Crumble – healthier than it sounds, the dish includes baked apple and mixed berries with quinoa, coconut crumb and honey cinnamon labneh. Whether you're trying to eat healthily or looking to indulge, there's something for everyone.

Lunch time?

There's also a delicious range of dishes available for lunch, whether you're looking for a light bite to eat or something more substantial. From the usual café favourites such as sandwiches and salads, there are also a number of Asian-style dishes such as Yakitori chicken salad, Galbi steak burger, and Mongolian lamb burger. Plus the ever-popular pulled pork sliders and G-Tree’s special fried chicken. So if you feel like something a bit different to your usual cafe order, you've come to the right place.
